About

Ovation will be a new concept beverage and food venue at the Encore. We will be offering innovative handcraft espresso creations, aromatic Moroccan mint tea, spiced Moroccan coffee and espresso, local and fresh pastries, snacks, sandwiches, a gluten free and kids menu. It will be paired with the highest quality service for the discerning palates of the populaces. Ovation will offer one stop convenience for this exquisite residential area, and the Pearl will benefit from this unique coffee, tea, and snack venue. We look forward to building a valuable relationship between Ovation and the community.
